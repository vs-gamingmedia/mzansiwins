World Sports Betting Monday Wheel Extended Until May 2026 - Over R10 Million Given Away

Promotions 14 March 2026 By Sipho Nkosi

World Sports Betting has extended its popular Monday Wheel promotion until 31 May 2026 after the original run proved so successful that over R10 million in World Coins has already been given away.

How it works

Every Monday, log in to your World Sports Betting account and spin the wheel for free. Each spin awards a random prize in World Coins (free bets), and every spin also enters you into the R50,000 cash prize draw at the end of the promotion.

There is no catch and no deposit required to spin. It is one free spin per account per week, available every Monday during the promotional period.

How to claim your prize

  1. Log in every Monday
  2. Spin the wheel
  3. Click the claim button that appears after spinning
  4. Free bets are credited to your World Coins balance (may take up to 30 minutes)

Important: you must click the claim pop-up button on the Monday you spin. If you miss it, the prize is forfeited.

R50,000 cash prize draw

On top of the weekly free bets, every spin enters you into a grand prize draw for R50,000 in cash. The draw takes place on 2 June 2026, and the winner will be contacted within 48 hours. The more Mondays you spin, the more entries you accumulate.

Key terms

  • Promotion runs from 24 November 2025 to 31 May 2026
  • One free spin per account per Monday
  • FICA-verified accounts only
  • Free bet turnover: 5x on sports, 30x on casino
  • R50,000 cash draw on 2 June 2026
  • Account must be active and in good standing

Is it worth it?

It is a free spin with zero cost to you, so the answer is straightforward. Even if your prize is a small free bet, it is still free value on top of your normal betting activity. The R50,000 cash draw is the real incentive to log in consistently every Monday.
