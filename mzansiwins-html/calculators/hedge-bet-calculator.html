How Hedge Betting Works

A hedge bet is a second wager placed against your original bet to reduce risk or guarantee profit. It is commonly used when your original bet is in a strong position and you want to secure some return rather than risk losing everything.

When to Hedge

  • Last leg of an accumulator - your multi bet needs one more result, hedge the opposite to guarantee profit
  • Pre-match to in-play - you backed a team pre-match, they are leading, hedge on the draw or opponent
  • Outright markets - you backed a team to win the league early in the season and they are now in the final
  • Changed circumstances - key player injured, conditions changed, you want to lock in value

Calculating Your Hedge

The calculator determines the optimal hedge stake to equalise profit across all outcomes, or you can adjust to weight one outcome higher if you prefer.

SA Betting Context

Quick Example

You placed R100 at 5.00 on a team to win. Before the match you can hedge at 1.50 on the other side to guarantee profit regardless of the result.
