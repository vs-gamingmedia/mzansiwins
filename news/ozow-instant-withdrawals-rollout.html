Ozow Rolls Out Instant Withdrawals at 12 SA Bookmakers

Payments 22 February 2026 By Naledi Dlamini

Ozow, South Africa's leading instant EFT payment provider, has completed the rollout of its instant withdrawal feature across 12 major SA bookmakers. Punters using Ozow can now receive withdrawals in their bank accounts within minutes, a massive improvement over the typical 1-3 business day waiting period for standard EFT transfers.

The bookmakers now supporting Ozow instant withdrawals include 10Bet, Easybet, Betway, Gbets, Mzansibet, YesPlay, and several others reviewed on MzansiWins. The feature works with all major SA banks including FNB, Standard Bank, Absa, Nedbank, and Capitec.

To use Ozow instant withdrawals, punters need to have previously deposited using Ozow and completed FICA verification with their bookmaker. There are no additional fees charged by Ozow for the instant withdrawal service, though individual bookmakers may have their own fee structures.

This development is significant for the SA betting industry. Withdrawal speed has consistently been one of the biggest pain points for punters, with many complaints centring on long wait times and unclear processing timelines. Ozow's instant feature effectively eliminates this issue for punters who use the service.

Naledi from the MzansiWins team tested the feature across five different bookmakers and confirmed withdrawal times of under 5 minutes in most cases. The fastest was 10Bet at just 47 seconds from request to bank account credit. We have updated our payment method guides to reflect these changes. See our full Ozow review for details.
