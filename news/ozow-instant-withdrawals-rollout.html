Ozow Rolls Out Instant Withdrawals at 12 SA Bookmakers

Payments 22 February 2026 By Naledi Khumalo

Ozow is quickly becoming the default withdrawal method for South African betting sites. The instant EFT service now operates across a growing number of licensed operators, and it is changing what punters expect from payout speeds.

How Ozow works

Ozow connects directly to your bank's internet banking system to process instant EFT payments. No card details, no third-party wallets. You authenticate through your bank, and the money moves. For withdrawals, the process is reversed - funds land in your bank account within minutes instead of the 1-3 business days that traditional EFT requires.

Who supports it

Major operators including Betway, Hollywoodbets, Easybet, and Mzansibet now offer Ozow for both deposits and withdrawals. Compatibility covers FNB, Standard Bank, Absa, Nedbank, and Capitec - the big five that account for the vast majority of SA banking customers.

Real-world payout times

In our testing across multiple operators, Ozow withdrawals typically clear in 5-20 minutes once the request is approved. The approval step is where delays can occur - some operators auto-approve repeat withdrawals, while others manually review each request during business hours.

The bigger picture

Instant payouts are becoming a baseline expectation rather than a premium feature. Operators that still take 48 hours to process withdrawals are going to lose customers to those offering Ozow. If fast cashouts matter to you, prioritise operators that support it.
